Dr. Ashlea Drakeford Dentist

Dr. Ashlea Drakeford leads the team of dental care experts here at For You Dental. Dr. Drakeford started For You Dental with a vision in mind, i.e., to provide quality dental care for neighboring communities. From then on, For You Dental has been providing high quality and compassionate dental service in Dallas, Texas, throughout the years. Dr. Drakeford has years of education, training and experience under her belt. She is an expert in general, cosmetic and sedation dentistry. She is also skilled in performing advanced procedures in dentistry that aren’t offered anywhere else. As a mother of 7-year-old twins, Cameron and Bryson, and as a loving wife for 9 years to her husband, Sheldon, Dr. Drakeford knows the importance of oral health in a family. She replicates the dental care she gives her family to her patients by providing a friendly and personal dental care.
